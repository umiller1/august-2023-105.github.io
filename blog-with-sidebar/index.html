Tenten Throws Shurikens of Shade: Fallout with Beyoncé After Renaissance Tour Drama

Temari and Beyoncé seemingly have developed an unbreakable bond much to Tenten's dislike.

by Ada Wong

Feb 2nd, 2024

In a turn of events that has Konohagakure buzzing, Tenten, the unassuming weapons specialist from Konoha, has found herself at the center of a storm following a fallout with none other than the Queen Bee herself, Beyoncé Knowles. The source of the drama? The Renaissance Tour, where Temari, the fierce ninja from the Sand Village, unexpectedly opened for Beyoncé's Konohagakure concert.

Fans were initially excited to see two powerful women share the stage, but little did they know that a ninja storm was brewing behind the scenes. Tenten, known for her stoic demeanor and deadly accuracy with tools, apparently did not take kindly to the surprise opening act. Rumors suggest that she had harbored dreams of showcasing her own impressive weapon skills to the world, and Beyoncé's choice to bring in Temari left her feeling betrayed.

The fallout reportedly started backstage, where Tenten confronted Beyoncé with a barrage of questions sharper than her own shurikens. Witnesses claim that Tenten demanded answers, asking why she was snubbed in favor of a foreign ninja from the Sand Village. Beyoncé, caught off guard, attempted to smooth things over, praising Temari's skills and emphasizing the importance of diversity on her tour.

“ Beyoncé knows that if a kunoichi beef with me, we gonna beef forever. Periodt. I don't take kindly to her allowing Temari, Who broke my back during the Chunin Exams, to take the stage in my home village. ” - Tenten

However, Tenten was having none of it. Eyewitnesses claim she retorted with, "I can handle weapons better than anyone, and I would've brought the house down!" The tense exchange escalated, with Tenten allegedly throwing metaphorical shurikens of shade at Beyoncé for not recognizing her ninja prowess.

The fallout didn't end there. Tenten took to social media to express her disappointment, posting cryptic messages and ninja-themed memes that hinted at her feelings of betrayal. The fandom erupted, with fans taking sides and creating a whirlwind of memes and fan art depicting Tenten as a misunderstood ninja warrior.

Beyoncé, known for her grace under pressure, has yet to officially comment on the situation. However, sources close to her camp suggest that she's hoping to mend fences and maybe even collaborate with Tenten in the future. Only time will tell if Tenten and Beyoncé can reconcile their differences and bring harmony back to the world of entertainment. Until then, fans can't help but wonder if this unexpected feud will inspire a new hit single from Queen Bey – perhaps a chart-topping anthem about the perils of ninja drama.
