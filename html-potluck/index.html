Immunity Cube

PREP TIME: 15 min · TOTAL TIME: 15 min · YIELDS: 15 Ice Cubes

A nutritious cube packed with vitamin infini and anti-sukuna ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of pure water
  • 1/4 cup raw honey
  • Juice of 1 whole watermelon
  • Juice of 2 whole oranges
  • 3-4 pieces of raw ginger
  • 3 pieces of raw turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Limitless CT

Instructions

  1. To a food processor, add all the ingredients above and blend until completely smooth and bright yellow/orange/red
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cube tray mold of my face and set in the freezer overnight.
  3. Transfer to an airtight container to store in the freezer and use whenever needed.
