Daily Fun Fact

— The color of your bread tag has a meaning. The varying colors indicate which day of the week the bread was baked. The color system is even alphabetical: Monday is blue, Tuesday is green, Thursday is red, Friday is white, and Saturday is yellow. Traditionally, bread wasn’t delivered on Wednesday or Sunday. This system is more for internal use among employees.
